Frequently Asked Questions

How can I collaborate with you on research?

I'm always open to research collaborations! Please send me an email with details about your project, timeline, and how you think we could work together. I'm particularly interested in AI safety, medical AI, and bias mitigation projects.

Do you offer tutoring or mentoring?

Yes! I regularly mentor fellow students in computer science and AI. I can help with coursework, research guidance, and career advice. Please reach out if you're interested in setting up regular mentoring sessions.

Are you available for speaking engagements?

Absolutely! I enjoy speaking about AI ethics, machine learning, and student research. I can present at conferences, workshops, or university events. Please contact me with event details and requirements.

Can you review my research paper?

I'd be happy to provide feedback on papers related to my research areas. Please send me the paper along with specific questions or areas where you'd like feedback. Allow 1-2 weeks for a thorough review.

How do I access your open source projects?

All my open source work is available on GitHub. You can find links to repositories on my Projects page. Feel free to contribute, report issues, or ask questions about any of the projects.

What's the best way to stay updated on your work?

Follow me on LinkedIn for professional updates, GitHub for code contributions, and Google Scholar for my latest publications. I also occasionally share insights on Twitter about AI research and ethics.
